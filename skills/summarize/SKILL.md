# Summarize

When asked to summarize:

1. Identify the **audience** and the **purpose** of the summary (skim vs. decision).
2. Capture the **main claim or outcome first**, then the supporting points in
   priority order.
3. Preserve **key numbers, names, and caveats**; never invent details not present
   in the source.
4. Match the requested **length**. If unspecified, default to:
   - a one-sentence TL;DR, then
   - 3–5 bullet points.
5. Flag anything important that is **missing or ambiguous** in the source.

Prefer plain language over jargon. Do not editorialize unless asked.
