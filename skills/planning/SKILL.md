# Planning

1. **Clarify the goal** and the definition of done. Surface unknowns early.
2. **Break it down** into concrete steps small enough to act on and check.
3. **Order by dependency** — what must come first; what can run in parallel.
4. **Make each step verifiable:** state how you'll know it worked.
5. **Flag risks and assumptions** and where the plan might need to change.
6. **Keep it lean:** include the recommended path, not every alternative.

A good plan is scannable in a minute and executable without re-deciding everything. Revisit
and adjust it as you learn, rather than following a stale plan off a cliff.
